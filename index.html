pyLoad Download Manager

Fast, lightweight and full featured download manager


Download pyLoad 0.5

About

Started as an experiment to avoid annoying wait time at Rapidshare & co, developet pyLoad to a powerfull downloader with many features and support for all most common and uncommen hosters, crypters and video platforms. It aims for low hardware requirements and platform independence to be runnable on casual Windows, Mac and Linux Systems as well as on routers, NAS and Servers. Furthermore to be flexible, it is controllable with a webinterface, terminal, Andoird/iOS App and even IRC and XMAPP. Aswell a goal is to safe resources, so it is very lightwidht and fast.

Features

Hosters & Crypters
    More than 120 hosters and 50 crypters are supported as well as over 60 premium hoster accounts

Flexible
    as entire written in Python, easy installable on local Windows, Mac or Linux System, NAS, Server and Router

Remote Control
    manage your downloads via a Webinterface, Terminal, Android or iOS App, IRC or XMPP

Captcha Recognition
    Automatically solve captchas or use CaptchaTrader

Plugins
    Unpack archives, testing Checksums, download scheduler, reconnets, external scripts, Click'n'Load and many more

Lightwight
    pyLoad needs very low resources of RAM and HDD

Easy to extend
    offers an easy API to create own plugins and share with the community

Community
    helpfull and friendly support in the IRC channel and forum

Open Source
    released under GPL license, all sources avaible at github
